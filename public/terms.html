PantryPal Terms

Last updated: 2026-05-17

These terms describe the current PantryPal launch behavior. PantryPal helps users track pantry items, grocery lists, recipes, meal plans, household preferences, and AI Chef meal suggestions.

Informational Use

PantryPal is for general food tracking and meal planning. PantryPal is not medical, nutrition, allergy, or dietary advice. Users are responsible for reviewing ingredients, allergens, nutrition estimates, expiration dates, and cooking instructions before preparing or eating food.

User Data And Guest Mode

Guest mode stores data locally in the browser on the current device. Guest data may be lost if browser storage is cleared, a different browser is used, or the device is changed. Users can export a backup from Settings > Data > Export JSON.

Accounts And Cloud Sync

Signed-in accounts use Supabase authentication and cloud sync. Users are responsible for keeping account credentials private and for signing out on shared devices.

AI Chef And Recipe Suggestions

AI Chef may use pantry items, expiring items, preferences, recipe data, and meal history to suggest meals. Suggestions may be incomplete or inaccurate. Review all ingredients, substitutions, cooking steps, and nutrition estimates before relying on them.

PantryPal Plus And Subscriptions

PantryPal Plus is the planned premium subscription for advanced AI Chef suggestions, Fridge Rescue, shared household barcode memory, and future receipt scanning support. If subscriptions are enabled on iOS or Android, purchases, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled by the applicable app store.

Users should manage or cancel subscriptions through their Apple App Store or Google Play account settings. PantryPal will include a Restore purchases option where required by the app stores.

Acceptable Use

Do not misuse PantryPal, attempt to disrupt the service, upload harmful content, reverse engineer protected services, or use the app in a way that violates applicable laws or platform rules.

Service Changes

PantryPal features may change as the app moves through launch, TestFlight, Google Play testing, and public release. Some planned features, including receipt scanning and richer nutrition support, may not be available at launch.
